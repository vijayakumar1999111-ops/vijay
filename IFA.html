Eligibility & Equivalence

Apollo University welcomes students from across the globe. Academic qualifications are reviewed for equivalence to national standards, ensuring fairness and transparency.

  • Recognized certificates and transcripts required
  • Equivalence certificates may be requested
  • English proficiency (IELTS/TOEFL) mandatory
